# Hamburger soup

## Ingredients

* 2 Tbsp butter
* 1 c chopped onion
* 1 c sliced carrot
* 1/2 c chopped jalapeños
* 1 lb ground beef
* 2 c tomato juice \(or V8\)
* 1 1/2 tsp salt
* 1/8 tsp pepper
* 1/2 cup flour
* 4 cups milk

## Steps

* Sauté onion, carrot, peppers
* Add beef
* Add juice, potato, salt and pepper
* Cook 20-25 minutes
* Beat flour with 1 c milk; add.
* Add rest of milk
* Heat throughout
