# Helen's Broccoli Soup

## Ingredients

* 1 medium diced onion
* 1 large diced potato
* 2/3 c diced celery
* 1 minced garlic
* 1/2 tsp pepper
* 3 cup chopped broccoli
* 5 cup chicken broth
* 1/4 tsp dried basil
