# Macaroni and Cheese

### Ingredients

* 1/2 lb macaroni \(2c\)
* 2.5-3 Tbsp butter
* 2 Tbsp flour
* 1 tsp dry mustard
* 1/2 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp white pepper
* 1 1/2 c milk
* 2 c cheese \(7-8 oz\)
* 1 small onion

### 6X ingredients

* 3 lb macaroni
* 2 sticks butter
* 3/c flour
* 1/8 c mustard
* 2 Tbsp salt
* 9 c milk
* 3 lbs cheese
* 2 large onions
