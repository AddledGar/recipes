# Keller chicken

Ingredients

* 1 chicken
* Salt

Steps

2. Truss the chicken \([https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4o7PdYDyDy8](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4o7PdYDyDy8)\)
3. Salt chicken
