# Thai chicken lettuce wraps

## Ingredients

* 1 lb ground chicken
* 1 Tbsp olive oil
* 2 Tbsp red curry paste
* 1 Tbsp ginger, minced
* 4 cloves garlic, minced
* 1 red bell pepper, thinly sliced
* 4 green onions, chopped
* 1 cup shredded cabbage or coleslaw mix
* 1/4 cup hoisin sauce
* salt and pepper
* 5 basil leaves, chopped
* 1/2 head iceberg lettuce cut into half \(or butter lettuce, or...\)

## Steps

* Add olive oil to large skillet and heat until oil is very hot. Add ground chicken and cook until no longer pink and starts to brown. Break up with a wooden spoon as necessary.  About 3 minutes
* Add red curry paste, ginger, garlic, peppers, coleslaw mix and stir-fry for about 3 minutes
* Add hoisin sauce and green onions, and toss.  Remove from heat, add basil and toss.  Transfer to a bowl
* Serve by placing spoonfuls of chicken in pieces of lettuce, fold and eat.
