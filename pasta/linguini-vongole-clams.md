# Linguini Vongole \(clams\)

## Ingredients

* 1/2 to 3/4 lb linguini
* 1 1/2 lb clams
* 8 garlic cloves
* lots of parsley, chopped
* 1/2 cup white wine
* butter & olive oil

## Steps

1. Cook garlic in olive oil & butter
2. Add white wine, heat
3. Add clams, cover until all have popped \(if some refuse to pop, throw them out\)
4. Add parsley, serve.
