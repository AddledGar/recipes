## Ingredients

* 2c oatmeal
* 2c flour
* 1/2c brown sugar
* 1 c butter
* 1 tsp soda
* 3/4 tsp salt
* 1/2 tsp vanilla
* 1/2 c sour milk (aka milk + 1 Tbsp vinager or lemon)

## Steps

* crumble shortening/butter
* add mixed dry ingredients
* add milk + mix
* let sit/chill until easy to handle
* bake 20m at 350 deg F
